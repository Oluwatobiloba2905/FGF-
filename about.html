About Us

About the Church

Friends of God fellowship is open to worshippers of all ages and backgrounds. We lead our congregation in worship, deepening the connection to God, understanding the teachings of Christ, and ultimately to a vibrant faith. Through the dedication of our leadership, members and volunteers, we are committed to the teachings of Jesus Christ, and are here to spread His message. Contact us today to find out more about becoming part of the Friends of God fellowship community. Welcome to the warm embrace of the Friends of God fellowship, where the doors are wide open for worshippers of all ages and backgrounds.

In this sacred space, we gather as a united congregation, intertwining our voices in heartfelt worship, as we strive to deepen our connection with the Divine. Here, the teachings of Christ are brought to life, illuminating our minds and hearts, and leading us towards a faith that thrives with vibrancy and vitality. Within our fellowship, you will find a family of dedicated souls who share a profound commitment to the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our leadership, members, and volunteers pour their hearts and souls into creating an environment where His message can truly flourish. Guided by unwavering devotion, they tirelessly strive to spread the transformative power of His love, kindness, and wisdom to all who seek solace and spiritual growth. As you step into our community, you will be enveloped by a sense of belonging, love, and acceptance. We believe that every individual is a precious thread in the tapestry of our fellowship, each one contributing a unique perspective and story that enriches our collective journey.

Together, we embark on a sacred pilgrimage, exploring the depths of faith, and supporting one another as we navigate life's challenges with grace and resilience. At the Friends of God fellowship, we invite you to discover the profound joy of fellowship and the transformative power of faith. It is a place where souls are nourished, hearts are uplifted, and spirits are renewed. Here, we not only worship together but forge deep and lasting connections, creating a community that is rooted in love, compassion, and unwavering support. If you feel the gentle tug of your soul leading you towards something greater, we encourage you to take that leap of faith and reach out to us today. Allow us to walk alongside you on your spiritual journey, as we explore the depths of worship, dive into the teachings of Christ, and experience the boundless blessings that await within the Friends of God fellowship community. May your heart be filled with anticipation as you take this step towards a deeper connection with God and a community that will embrace you with open arms. Together, let us embark on a transformative path, where our faith is ignited, our spirits are uplifted, and our lives are forever changed.

Our Vision

VISION

Friends of God fellowship is an inclusive church committed to Christ and His word. We are dedicated to Restoring the ancient Landmarks, Lifting up a standard and raising kingdom builders. Our church is not just a house of worship. At its core are the members and believers that come together to practice God’s word, celebrate His love and extend His gifts to all.
